Hello I am Thaisha Rivera

Freelance creative/ aspiring front-end developer based in the US.

Graduated in 2013 from Albright College with a Bachelor of Arts in Design and Merchandising. I was fortunate enough to intern for NY- based "zero-waste" designer Daniel Silverstein my senior year at Albright. I worked as Starbucks barista trainer/supervisor in a ridiculously busy rest stop for 10 1/2 years until the pandemic hit. I have a great love for anything that requires artistry which prompted me to start Thaisha's Creations in 2015. Through Thaisha's Creations I offer design, sewing and other crafting services to supplement my income.
